The Walker Club

Reading a book is a serious and important endeavor that takes time and effort. Wouldn't you like to spend less time reading and more time solving your problems?

—

Book discussions without the work

Want to participate in discussions about books without having to read them? We'll discuss it all with you—in-depth, insightful readings that leave you satisfied. All you have to do is send us your thoughts on the book, not the whole thing!

—

No more excuses for not reading

There are new books published every day and people are always telling themselves they'll read them later, and then later becomes never. With The Walker Club, don't worry about putting off the classics because we'll do it with you!

—

No need to buy new books

With The Walker Club's monthly subscription plan, you won't have to worry about getting new books because we'll send them straight to your doorsteps before their release date! Let us read them for you instead of wasting precious space on your shelves.
